About


Today, individuals find it difficult to share their thoughts with people, especially online. Whether it’s an announcement about their lives, a prediction which they choose to make or a confession about something, Prefute offers users a safe way to add these “prefutes” on their website, either anonymously or otherwise and gives all consumers a way to express themselves freely. The entire world revolves around the system of assumptions, big public announcements and predictions that individuals make on either a personal scale or a professional one. By putting these prefutes online, we are giving our audience a chance to see what is new and happening in the world and also a safe place to express what one may be going through.

Our website allows you to show the world what interests you, what makes you happy and even play an adventurous betting game with friends based on your predictions. Scrolling through our website, you will find topics that interest the general public and topics that are trending. You will further find topics which we think are of interest to you to provide you with an overall interactive and fun experience. At prefute, we aim to open the floodgates to the world. Our mission is to provide a safe and secure environment for users to express themselves openly and make friends along the way.
